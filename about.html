My Story

A path that found me before I found it

I didn't set out to become a healer. Like a lot of people, I arrived here through burnout — years of pushing through exhaustion, disconnected from my own body, until a single Reiki session cracked something open I didn't know was closed.

That session changed the direction of my life. I spent the following years training formally, sitting in stillness, and slowly building a practice rooted in presence rather than performance. Today, my work is simple: to hold space for people to reconnect with the light that stress, grief, and busyness have dimmed — never extinguished.

Every session is different, because every person who walks through the door is different. What stays the same is the care, the quiet, and the trust that your body already knows how to heal — it just needs the room to do it.

Philosophy

Healing isn't something done to you — it's something remembered within you. My role is simply to help clear the path.
